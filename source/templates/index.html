Welcome to our PennApps 2018 Hack!

Our app, titled CYA uses natural language processing to predict the typical response to a controversial social media post. This is not intended to encourage self censorship. On the contrary, we want people to feel at ease when putting their opinion out there. If users know what to expect when making their post, they will be less anxious about voicing their opinion on the internet. Thus, we aim to ease user's anxiety about posting on social media.
